Rank Coders in the UAE

Why is the ranking system important:

Ranking coders is important to map out your coding skills. This is because, the rank conveys multiple points of information, such as a coder improvement points, these improvement points acts as feedback loop to the coder, which continuously acts as a motivation to enhance a coder individual ability.

Benefits of being enlisted:

  1. Know your level in coding
  2. Use your score as strength point when getting hired to a coding-related occupation.

Upskill Coders in the UAE

Being continuously upskilled in various technical areas is extremely important, especially that technologies now days, have a short lifespan. After attaining your score (via ranking system), fill up your gaps and fill up your curiosity with our various upskilling courses provided by world-leading companies.

Benefits:

  1. Being continuously aware of new coding tools
  2. Exposure to the world best practices in your coding field
  3. Meet interesting individuals in your industry
