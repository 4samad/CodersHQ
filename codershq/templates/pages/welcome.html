About Us

Enabling coders, coding communities, and anyone who wants to code

Coders HQ was built based on enabling coders, coding communities, and anyone who wants to code. This initiative was formulated based on supporting coders, who work as strategic enablers to the UAE digital economy.

The coders HQ platform was built with passion, it was built by the community and for the community. The coders HQ platform development involved in its core the local coding community to provide for them the means to be part of something bigger to contribute on what is the for the local coding community and coding communities all around the world.
