About Us

A Small Team With A Dream To Code

Coders HQ is built with the love and passion of a really small team of coders. The development started in November of 2020 and changed a lot since then. We started with 10 volunteers which reduced to 5 and these 5 became the founding fathers of the project. We went through different cycles and recreated the code base more than once until we achieved something resembling a stable source code.

What kept us going is the soul of Coders HQ. This project aims to literally change the direction of programmers in the UAE by giving them the tools and the ability to showcase themselves without the need of anything but pure raw talent and dedication.

Lastly, The final project of coder (HQ) – includes combining most of the initiatives together in 1 initiative.

This initiative is called: 021

021 is a program that takes the whole process form ideation to commercialization. This happens through incubating 6-8 individuals, who are recent graduates, an take them through the whole process from ideating the idea à developing the idea into a project à commercializing the product itself. This program will have an immense impact building the mentality of individuals, by taking them through the whole business cycle and support them with resources to enable them to reach to the program goal.

The program will help individuals, through enabling them to grow their hard skills and their soft skills together.

This program is limited for Emirati individuals – and requires an investment from a corporation.

Emerging talents to be the talent the disrupt industries. In the coding field, coding talent needs to be nurtured and fostered from the beginning.

This is why coders HQ launches : (HQ) internships.

This internship program is designed for fresh university undergraduates and is offered by companies and corporations that are considered pioneers in their fields. Members in Coders (HQ) will be able to participate in an internship, via registering and applying through the coders HQ platform.

Entities will have access to some of the best-skilled students in the technical field, selecting interns based on their skills through a wide variety of options. Corporations on the other hand will be able to choose the talents they demand, additionally to that distribute what they are specifically looking for in talents.
